About Me

I have always had a passion for design and after graduating from the University of Brighton with a degree in Print Design and Business Studies, I have been working at a fashion company as the assistant print designer, recently moving in to a role supporting the wider design team, in particular embroidery and embellishment. Both of which I design using Photoshop and Illustrator at a professional level.

I have loved this type of design however I wanted a new challenge that could develop and combine my creativity and technical skill. I am an enthusiastic person who is always eager to learn new things. I therefore have pursued a career in front-end development, with a particular interest in CSS, HTML5 and JavaScript.

In November 2016 I joined the Blended Professional Program with Just IT whilst maintaining my full time job. This self-funded course has been an exciting new challenge and a step towards a new career in front-end development. During my time as a designer I understand how important it is to keep challenging yourself and your technical skill.
